Our Dream

A computer that works for you — and only you.

The most powerful technology of our era is being built to watch us, harvest us, and sell us back to ourselves. Every "assistant" is a microphone into someone else's data center. Your thoughts, your documents, your habits — all of it streamed away, mined, and monetized.

We believe it can be the opposite. AI should be personal — running on hardware you own, answering to you alone. It should make you more capable without making you more surveilled. It should be something you own, not something you rent and are rented out by.

ThorOS is our attempt to build that. An operating system where the intelligence lives on your machine, the code is open for anyone to read, and the only person your computer reports to is you. No accounts. No telemetry. No cloud you didn't ask for.

It's named in the Norse spirit — Yggdrasil, the world-tree whose roots hold everything together; Thor, who stands guard. A foundation for digital sovereignty, built for the people who still believe a computer should be a tool for human freedom, not a leash.

Where we're headed

Today ThorOS is a working preview: a voice-first desktop with agents that genuinely operate the machine. Tomorrow it's a platform — a place where anyone can build an agent that masters a program, share a personality that reshapes the whole system, and download capabilities the way you'd download an idea.

It's early, it's open, and it's built in the open. If any of this resonates with you, come build it with us.
