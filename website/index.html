ThorOS

The Tree of Knowledge · The Root of Intelligence · The Future of Freedom

An AI-first, local-first operating system. Talk to it, and it gets real work done — opening apps, writing and editing documents, running commands — entirely on your own machine. No cloud. No tracking. Powered by Yggdrasil AI.

What is ThorOS?

A complete desktop operating system, built on Debian Linux, where the assistant isn't an app you open — it is the way you use the computer.

You speak (or type) a request in plain language. An on-device planner figures out what you mean and hands it to specialized agents — for files, applications, documents, the terminal, and more — that carry it out and report back. "Open my receipts document." "Make this bold and export it to PDF." "What was I working on yesterday?" It just does it.

Everything — the speech recognition, the language model, the voice — runs 100% on your hardware. Unplug the internet and it still works. Your documents, your commands, and your conversations never leave the machine.

Why it's different

Most "AI assistants" send your life to someone else's servers. ThorOS doesn't.
