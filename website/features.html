Features

ThorOS is an assistant that actually operates your computer — locally, safely, and out loud. Here's what's inside the preview.

🎙️

A fully local voice assistant

Say the wake word, then your request. Wake-word detection, speech-to-text (Whisper), the language model (via Ollama), and the spoken voice (Piper) all run on your own GPU — no audio or text ever leaves the machine.

🧠

An orchestrator that plans, and agents that act

Your words go to an on-device planner that decides what to do and routes it to specialized agents: Files, Apps (open, close, browse, search), Documents, the Terminal, Memory, Security, and more. Each agent does one job well.

🎯

Context-aware — it knows what you're looking at

Open a terminal and say "list files" and it runs ls right there. Focus a document and "make it bold" formats it. The same words do the right thing depending on the window in front of you.

📄

Deep control of real applications

Beyond clicking menus, ThorOS speaks LibreOffice's own command language. "Replace michael with Michael," "insert a page break," "export this to PDF" — driven through the application's real API, not fragile screen automation.

💬

An AI that explains itself

Ask "why did you do that?" and it tells you the genuine reasoning behind its last action — what you asked, what it saw, and what it chose — reconstructed from the real decision, not a made-up story.

🧷

It remembers you

Tell it your name, your preferences, the way you work, and it remembers across sessions — stored in a plain file on your disk that you own and can read.

⌨️

Type or talk

Prefer to type? A chat window gives you the exact same assistant and agents, for quiet rooms or when your hands are on the keyboard.

🛡️

You're in control

Routine actions just happen; anything risky — deleting files, running system commands — is gated behind an authorization code, with a denylist that refuses catastrophic commands. An always-on guardian watches for trouble in the background.

🧩

Modular — install capabilities, not software

The whole system is built from swappable agents and personalities. The long-term goal is a community marketplace: download an agent that deeply controls your favorite program, or a personality that turns ThorOS into a specialist for the week.

🌐

Works offline. Scales up when you want.

Everything runs on-device by design. The architecture also supports an optional cloud brain or a thin-client mode for lighter hardware — opt-in, never required, never a fork.
